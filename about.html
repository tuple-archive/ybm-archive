about

“I would recommend YBM without hesitation. They deliver outstanding, award-winning editorial that delights and engages readers.”

YBM’s team create award-winning publications for the world’s leading institutions.

YBM is a multiple award-winning content marketing agency. We build powerful relationships with readers and customers through creating imaginative, multilayered branded content online and in print. We marshal exceptional creativity and rigorous project management to this end.

Our philosophy is simple: we believe branded content can compete with national newspapers and magazines. We recognise that readers are bombarded with content, and that most goes directly from the doormat to the recycling bin, or from the inbox to the trash.

Breaking this cycle is not complicated. We believe outstanding words, pictures, audio and video will always succeed in grabbing and holding readers’ attention.

To achieve this, we draw on our extensive in-house experience on national magazines and newspapers, work with leading freelance writers, artists and photographers, and make use of sector-specific expertise, such as CASE, the Council for Advancement and Support of Education.

YBM offers a nose to tail service, delivering all aspects of production from initial scoping and research, through copy writing, editing, photography, web development, all the way to printing, fulfilment and mailing. And we are always on the end of the phone for advice or a chat.

Our publications have won ten CASE Awards in the past six years, including three Gold awards.
